Devastating Impacts and Effects of earthquakes

Click to move across the slideshow, or let the slideshow autoplay

In these photos, you can see how deadly and devastating earthquakes can be. You can also see the impacts and effects earthquakes leave on families, communities, towns, cities, and countries.





Devastation

In this photo, we can see the effects of an earthquake in Java, Indonesia. The earthquake had a magnitude of 7.7 and killed more than 500 people.

Destruction

In this photo, we see the effects of an earthquake in Mexico City. This was one of the deadliest earthquakes in North America, and still to this day, this region is hit hard with earthquakes.

Loss of Life

Here is a picture of an earthquake that hit the Phillippines in 2009. The earthquake was a 6.6 magnitude earthquake and caused structural collapse, displacing thousands of people. About 140 people died from this earthquake.

Oklahoma, 2011

This was the devastating earthquake of 2011 that hit Oklahoma. It was a 5.7 magnitude earthquake that destroyed buildings, collapsed farms, and caused many fires. It was the most powerful earthquake ever recorded in Oklahoma.
